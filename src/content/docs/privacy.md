DayZ Modders is a static documentation site, and it is intentionally minimal about data.

## What this site collects

- **No cookies.** The site sets no cookies and uses no client-side storage for tracking.
- **No analytics or tracking.** There are no analytics scripts, tag managers, or
  third-party trackers.
- **Search runs in your browser.** Site search is powered by
  [Pagefind](https://pagefind.app/), which runs entirely client-side — your queries
  are not sent to a server.

## Hosting

The site is hosted on **GitHub Pages**. As with any web host, GitHub's servers may
process technical information such as your IP address and request metadata in their
logs to deliver the site. This is governed by the
[GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

## External links

Pages may link to third-party services (e.g. Discord, GitHub, YouTube). Those services
have their own privacy practices, which this notice does not cover.

This notice may be updated as the site evolves. See the [Contact page](/contact/) to
get in touch.
